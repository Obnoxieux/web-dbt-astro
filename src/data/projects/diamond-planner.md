## Elevator pitch

There are probably thousands of apps on the market that market to sports teams to organize their activities. Most of them run a catch-all approach that tries to cover many different sports.

Diamond Planner focuses on German federation baseball exclusively, optimizing every bit of functionality for this use case. 
All games can be imported via the central German Baseball Federation API and kept in sync during the season - no manual event creation at the start of the season or catching up with scheduling changes.

### Status

A MVP is currently used by the Berlin Skylarks Baseball Club, general rollout to the public to follow.
